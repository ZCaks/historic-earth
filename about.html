About HEarththen

Earththen is a community-driven project to preserve and document historic photographs from around the world. Users can upload old images, tag them by year and location, and help build a visual archive of humanity's past.

Goals

  • 🗺️ Geolocate historical photos to their real-world coordinates
  • 📸 Add meaningful context (year, description, location accuracy)
  • 👥 Allow verified moderators to curate content
  • 📂 Offer search and filter functionality to explore world history visually

Who Runs This?

This site was lovingly built and is maintained by Žan, with contributions from passionate history enthusiasts. If you have suggestions, feedback, or just want to say hi, reach us at contact@earththen.net.
